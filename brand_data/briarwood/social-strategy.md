## Strategic Objective
Contribute to 60 qualified calls per month across all brands by positioning Briarwood as the urgent, safe, comfortable FIRST STEP in recovery.

## The Shift
FROM: Generic engagement content → no calls
TO: Crisis-moment, withdrawal-fear-busting, persona-targeted content → phone pickups

## Content Ratio Target (adjusted for detox urgency)
- 35% ToF (Early/60%) — Educational, normalize detox, signs it's time
- 35% MoF (Aware/20%) — Withdrawal fear, objection handling, family content
- 30% BoF (Ready+Comparing/20%) — Direct CTAs, beds available, insurance, Executive Detox
