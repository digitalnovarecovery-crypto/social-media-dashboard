## Strategic Objective
Generate 60 qualified calls per month from social media by shifting from vanity-metric content (motivational quotes, generic engagement) to awareness-level-mapped, persona-targeted content that drives phone pickups.

## The Shift
FROM: Generic motivational content → likes/comments → no calls
TO: Persona-specific, awareness-mapped content → right person sees right message → picks up phone

## Content Ratio Target
- 40% ToF (Early/60%) — Educational, normalize recovery, broad reach, no hard sell
- 40% MoF (Aware/20%) — Objection handling, persona pain points, family content
- 20% BoF (Ready+Comparing/20%) — Direct CTAs, testimonials, tours, insurance info
